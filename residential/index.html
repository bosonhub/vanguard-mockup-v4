Corvallis Residential Market

Corvallis is consistently ranked among the most livable small cities in Oregon. Proximity to OSU, a vibrant downtown, and access to outdoor recreation make it a highly desirable place to live — which keeps demand for residential real estate strong year-round.

Albany offers more affordable options with excellent access to Corvallis and the I-5 corridor, making it a popular choice for buyers who want more space for their budget.
